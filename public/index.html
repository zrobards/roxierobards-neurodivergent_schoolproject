Neurodiversity in a sentence

Neurodiversity is the idea that differences in thinking, learning, and sensing are expected parts of being human. It is not the same as mental illness. It is a perspective that values varied brain wiring and lived experiences.

Quick definitions

  • ADHD: differences in attention, pacing, and focus.
  • Autism: differences in communication, sensory processing, and social patterns.
  • Dyslexia: differences in reading and language processing.
  • Sensory processing: differences in how senses register and organize input.
  • OCD (briefly): cycles of intrusive thoughts and repetitive responses; not a personality quirk.

Supportive mindset

  • Assume competence and respect preferences.
  • Ask what helps instead of guessing.
  • Offer options: quiet spaces, clear instructions, time to process.
  • Celebrate strengths like creativity, pattern spotting, focus, and empathy.
